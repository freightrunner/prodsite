About Informed

Informed Hosted by Rob Lowe is an award-winning program that highlights new stories and innovative concepts through groundbreaking short-form and long-form documentary presentations. For nearly a decade this evolutionary series has been providing companies with the voice needed to educate the public and make a change in the world.

Public Television Documentary

Short-form documentary (3-5 minutes) will be distributed to Public Television stations in all 50 states, airing for one year, for unlimited broadcast at each stations’ discretion (estimated viewership and reach for one year is 60 million households). The short-form documentary is hosted by Rob Lowe.

National One Minute Education Commercial

A one minute educational commercial that airs one time nationwide across the major networks (CNN, MSNBC, Discovery, Fox Business, etc.) at prime time (6pm-10pm) and then an additional 400 times at prime time in the top 100 markets in the US – Reaches 80 million viewers

Regional One Minute Education Commercial

Informed will provide the production of one (1) high end one-minute educational commercial segment used for network distribution, broadcast fifty (50) times primetime via MSNBC, CNBC, CNN or an equivalent network, and in the city of your choice.

Corporate Profile

Informed will provide the production of one (1) broadcast quality, 5-6 minute educational documentary profile in HD with expansive and detailed information documenting the issues and educational message that concern your target audience.
