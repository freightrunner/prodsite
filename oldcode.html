Informed is an award winning short-form documentary series, hosted by Rob Lowe, which educates the public on a broad variety of subjects.

  • Each segment runs on average 3-5 minutes and bridge the gap between regularly scheduled programs on Public Television

  • Each segment features one organization, selected by our producers, that will help educate millions of viewers about a specific topic and industry.

  • The purpose of this documentary is strictly educational, not commercial.

  • The shortform piece will air on PTV for 1 year to roughly 60 million households.

    • 99% of US households receive Public Television Stations

    • Nearly 70% of American households watch Public Television
