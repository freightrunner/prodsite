Selection Process

Typical Selection Process

Though every segment we produce at Informed is unique, our selection process is typically the same for every story we consider:

  • Initial Interview: A chance for our Producer to learn more about a company's history, passions, ambitions, and what makes them unique. If the story appears to be a good fit for the Informed series, then a final interview with our VP of Programming will need to be scheduled. Prior to this final interview, the organization must email or mail any literature, videos, products and/or press releases that will assist our team in vetting your story.
    (*Typically, a 2-5 day process)

  • Final Vetting and Scheduling: Upon our executive team approving a story/organization; Informed will then extend an Invitation/Production Authorization that would need to be signed and returned for scheduling. That will officially stop our search for a story. Informed would then overnight the next steps of the project and the production cycle begins. (roughly 90-120 days)
